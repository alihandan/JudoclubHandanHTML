Welcome to Judo Club Handan!

About Us

Hey there! We’re Judo Club Handan, located at Antuna Hangija 1. We’re a small but awesome group of 15 judo lovers who want to share our passion for this cool martial art. Whether you’re a newbie or a pro, we’ve got a place for you!

Judo isn’t just about fighting; it’s about respect, discipline, and bettering yourself. We’re like a family here, and we help each other grow.

Why Join Us?

Joining our club means you’ll learn from experienced instructors in a fun and safe space. Whether you want to get fit, learn self-defense, or just try something new, we’re here for you!

We welcome everyone, no matter your age or skill level. Plus, you’ll build mental strength and make great friends along the way!

Training Schedule

We train every day from 20:00 to 21:30. Come join us and make the most of it! Our coaches are super friendly and always ready to help you improve.

If you want to check us out or try a class, just drop by during our training hours. We love meeting new faces!

Membership Info

Becoming a member is easy and costs only 50 KM a month. This gives you access to all our training sessions and events, plus some cool perks like coaching and gear.

We believe judo should be for everyone, so we offer flexible payment options and discounts for families and students. Join us and be part of our supportive community!
